About

Pavel Ljubicic began drumming at age four, inspired by his father’s kit and his namesake, the legendary Cozy Powell. Rooted in classic rock yet constantly evolving, his sound is shaped by influences from prog titans and Britpop innovators to the raw power of heavy metal.

A classically-trained musician with self-taught flair, Pavel is fluent across sheet music and improvisation. His session credits span singles, EPs, and albums across the USA, UK, Portugal, Italy, and beyond. He is currently pursuing a BA in Popular Music Performance & Event Management at BIMM University, Manchester.
